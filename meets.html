What are "Swim Meets"

Swim meets are gatherings of swimmers in order to compete against eachother. There are many different events that take place in them. Swimmers compete both individually and as a team. They get scores based on what place they get in and whoever has the most points can win high point! Also, those points go into the team scoring.

Different types of Swim Meets

There are many different types of swim meets:

    Club, USA, National, and Olympic
